Why use Longhorn?

Highly available persistent storage for Kubernetes

In the past, ITOps and DevOps professionals have found it hard to add replicated storage to Kubernetes clusters. As a result many non-cloud-hosted Kubernetes clusters don’t support persistent storage. External storage arrays are non-portable and can be extremely expensive.

Longhorn delivers easy to deploy, 100% open source, cloud-native persistent block storage without the cost overhead of open core or proprietary alternatives.

Easy incremental snapshots and backups

Longhorn’s scheduled snap and clone features make it possible for developers to work with the currently-active datastore from their production environments.

Performing regular backups of persistent storage volumes in Kubernetes clusters is simplified with Longhorn’s intuitive, free management UI.

Safe & reliable multi-cluster replication

External replication solutions will recover from a disk failure by re-replicating the entire data store. This can take days, during which time the cluster performs poorly and has a higher risk of failure.

Using Longhorn you can easily create a read-only remote replica volume in another Kubernetes cluster and fail over to it in the event of an emergency.

If your main cluster fails, Longhorn can set the replica to RW and bring up the app in the DR cluster quickly.
